About Us

Join us on this journey—sip, share, and explore with Kohi!


At Kohi, we are passionate about bringing coffee enthusiasts and independent specialty cafes together through a seamless and rewarding experience. Our mobile app connects you to a world of artisanal coffee by providing detailed profiles, locations, and reviews of the best specialty cafes. Whether you're a local coffee lover or exploring new cities, Kohi makes discovering unique cafes easier and more enjoyable.


With our integrated customer loyalty program, Kohi Select, you can earn and redeem rewards at participating cafes across different locations. We believe in building a vibrant coffee community, which is why Kohi includes interactive features like user-generated content, community discussions, and personalized recommendations.


Our mission is simple: to create meaningful connections between coffee lovers and independent cafes while supporting the growth of local coffee culture. Kohi celebrates the craftsmanship of specialty cafes and offers a tailored experience for those who appreciate high-quality coffee.


We are starting in the Greater Boston and New York City areas, with plans to expand globally. Kohi is set to become your go-to platform for discovering and enjoying the world of specialty coffee.
