Discover Unique Cafes

Finding exceptional specialty cafes can be a challenge, especially when traveling or exploring new areas. That's where Kohi comes in. Our app helps you easily discover the finest independent coffee shops, complete with curated details on each cafe's ambiance, menu, and customer ratings. No more scrolling through generic listings—Kohi connects you to the heart of the coffee world.

Explore, Rank, and Save Your Favorites

With Kohi’s Explore feature, you can browse cafes on a map, read detailed profiles, and even save your favorite spots for quick access. Our Cafes section ranks shops based on categories like best drinks, desserts, and ambiance, so you’ll always find the perfect match for your mood. Whether you're seeking a cozy nook or a bustling urban coffee bar, Kohi makes it easy to explore new coffee experiences.

Kohi isn’t just for consumers—it’s also a powerful tool for independent cafes. We help cafes grow by offering a cohesive loyalty program and a platform to reach a wider audience of coffee lovers.
