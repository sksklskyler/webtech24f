Designed by
enthusiasts,
for enthusiasts

At Kohi, we live and breathe coffee. Our founder has been on a journey from enjoying coffee at specialty cafés for nearly a decade, to crafting his own brews. Like many enthusiasts, she found it challenging to discover quality cafés—especially when exploring new cities. Tired of spending hours researching or ending up with a less-than-perfect cup, Kohi was born out of a desire to help coffee lovers find exceptional coffee experiences with ease.

Fostering
Community
& Connection

Kohi is more than an app. It’s a bridge between coffee lovers and independent cafés. We believe in creating a space where coffee enthusiasts can connect with local cafés that share their passion, fostering a community that celebrates the artistry and culture of coffee. By supporting these cafés, Kohi strengthens a network where both patrons and cafés thrive together.

Supporting
Independent
Cafes

The café industry is fiercely competitive. Many cafés struggle to stand out, with some going to great lengths, like hosting fashion shows, just to capture attention. Kohi exists to give these cafes a boost, helping them attract loyal customers who genuinely appreciate their craft. Our mission is to empower cafés not just to survive but to flourish, allowing them to focus on what they do best: delivering quality coffee experiences.

Educating
the Community

We’re passionate about helping people deepen their appreciation for specialty coffee. We want to include resources to educate coffee lovers on the nuances of coffee tasting, from understanding flavor profiles to identifying quality beans. Whether you’re a novice or a seasoned enthusiast, we guide you through the journey of tasting coffee with a discerning palate, so you can truly savor every cup and understand what makes it special.
